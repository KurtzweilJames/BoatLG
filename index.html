In the Water

Swimming
  • Boats must be anchored or idle manned with a competent person
  • Swimmers must remain within 50 feet of boat
  • Swimming is only allowed sunrise to sunset
Water Sking
  • There must be a minimum of two competent persons on the towing watercraft: one driver and one observer
  • Those being towed must be wearing a United States Coast Guard approved Personal Floatation Device (PFD)
SCUBA Diving
  • Divers must register with the Water Saftey Patrol
  • Minimum of Two Divers in the water
  • Boats must be manned, anchored, and have diver's flag
  • No diving in the bays between May 15 and September 15
  • Diving is only allowed in daytime
